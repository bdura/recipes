recipe:
  servings: 10 parts
  prep: 15 minutes
  cook: 20 minutes
  ingredients:
    - 200 g of black dessert chocolate
    - 150 g of sugar
    - 150 g of salted butter
    - 3 eggs
    - 50 g of flour
  directions:
    - Melt the chocolate with the butter, without over-heating it.
    - In the meantime, mix the sugar with the eggs.
    - Add the chocolate and butter mixture.
    - Mix in the flour.
    - "Bake at 180Â°C for around 20 minutes. If possible, use convection heating. Drive a knife in: when ready, the knife should come out almost clean."
