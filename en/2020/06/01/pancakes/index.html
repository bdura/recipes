Pancakes

June 1, 2020
18 pancakes
20 minutes
1 hour

Ingredients

  • 250 g of flour
  • 300 mL of milk
  • 65 g of butter
  • 30 g of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • one yeast portion
  • a pinch of salt

Directions

  1. Dilute the yeast in lukewarm milk.
  2. Mix the flour and the sugar, then add the egg and finally the butter.
  3. Gradually thin the mix down with the milk.
  4. Let the dough rest for at least an hour in the fridge.
  5. Get the pan to medium-low heat. If it is too hot, the pancakes will burn before the core is done...
